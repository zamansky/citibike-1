What

Click on a station and it will show you the current state as well as a graph showing the number of bikes over time.

I might add more graph options as more data is collected (single day, week graph, average for a particular day of the week).

Why

I wanted to play with an assortment of tools: d3.js, backbone.js, mongodb and this seemed like a neat little project.
